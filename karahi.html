Chicken Karahi

Ingredients:

  • 400 grams of chicken
  • 3/4 cup capsicums
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 3/4 cup tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 red chilli
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 cup onions chopped finely
  • 2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt or as needed
  • 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 10 cashew nuts
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi
  • 2 tablespoons coriander leaves
    •

    For karahi masala:

  • 1 1/2 coriander seeds
  • 4 red chillies
  • 1 to 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 3/4 teaspoons funnel seeds
    •

Recipe

  1. Heat pan with oil
  2. Fry cubed onion and capsicum for 2 to 3 minutes on high flame
  3. when they smell good, set them aside in a plate
  4. Add 2 red chilli and cumin
  5. When cumins begin to crackle add onions fry it till golden
  6. Time to add ginger garlic paste and fry till the raw smell disappears
  7. Add chicken and saute for 3 to 4 mins
  8. Cover and cook for low flame for 4 to 5 minutes. Donot add water.
  9. Add karahi masala, garam masala, red chilli and turmeric. I mixed up all of them and added here. Saute for another 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. Add tomato puree, cashew paste and salt
  11. Stir and cook uncovered till the raw smell of tomato puree go away. The gravy also thickens. This takes around 5 minutes on a medium to low flame.
  12. Cover and cook untill the chicken is soft and fully cooked.
  13. Add onions and capsicums
  14. Mix and cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  15. Pour 3 tbsp cream to a cup, add little tomato gravy from karahi to the cream. Mix and add back to the kadai
  16. Lastly I add sprankled crushed kasuri methi and coriander leaves. I also added a bit more of garan masala. Cooked for another two minutes.
  17. Garnish karahi chicken with coriander leaves, lemons, onion wedges and ginger julienne
  18. Chicken Karahi is ready :-)
